Autonomous Race cars 1/10 the scale, but its 10 times the fun

         

Week 4

1 Dec, 2015 Posted by ARC

    The demo last week went pretty well with car running through levine corridors on 2nd floor accuractely. The next and final task was dynamic obstacle avoidance. Basically, the car had to me made capable of avoiding any unknown obstacle on its way, detect it on time and manoeuver by its side without stopping.

     One of the major challenges was that we were using only LiDAR for orientation, distance and any other sensing. Adding one more task to this led to cross conditions matching and we had to make previous approaches more robust. For obstacle detection, we had to take in the scans in the front direction of the car and analyze the object dimensions. However the orientation of the car can change the sensing directions as well. In order to overcome this, the sensing direction was kept dynamic as per the orientation of the car, thus allowing us to sense in exact frontal region despite of the change in orientation of the car.

     Other challenge was object dimensions weren't fixed and we had to make sure we don't miss the object edges and thus dimensions and with a wrong estimate, bang into the object. To avoid this, we started object detection with a small central view and expanded it batch by batch if we found an object but couldn't find its edges. This allowed us to detect pretty accurate edges for the objects. The object dimensions and the distance of the edges from both the walls were measured.

     These distances were compared and the region having max distance was considered as the travelling lane and the navigation was shifted to the corresponsing lane. Once lane was changed, the car would go around the obstacle and then return to its original lane.

     We tested this algorithm on wide range of objects, and humans as well. And it worked perfectly as planned. (Though after a night full of bug fixing.) To test its limit, we moved around a 2nd car in the path of this autonomous car, to see if it can overtake it, and upto a certain speed limit of the 2nd car, the overtaking worked pretty good.

LiDAR, ROS, localization, obstacle avoidance
